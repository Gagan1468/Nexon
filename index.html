Product overview

One unified platform for capturing, verifying, and reporting attendance.

Nexon connects your cameras or kiosks to a central cloud dashboard, so admins see live presence, generate reports, and integrate with existing school or HR systems.

Face capture

Plug Nexon into existing CCTV or a dedicated tablet at your entry gate. Faces are captured passively as people walk in—no cards or fingerprints.

AI matching

Nexon’s engine validates faces against your enrolled users, even with masks, glasses, and lighting changes, and flags suspicious behaviour instantly.

Reporting & alerts

Generate daily, weekly, or monthly reports in one click, while parents and HR get real-time alerts for late entries or absences.

Built for your world

Tailored experiences for schools, colleges, and enterprises.
